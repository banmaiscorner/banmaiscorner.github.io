My name is Ban-Mai Anh Vo. I am a graphic designer from Phoenix area, Arizona. I design logos, brand identity, promotion items, and digital products. Graphic design to me is not only a job but also a lifestyle, which I enhance with tireless learning day after day.

I have been fortunate to work with clients from the Far East to the West Coast of America, which helps me grow my ability to adapt diverse cultures. I love creating visual solutions for communicating within the diversity of human beings in both print and digital. You can find me hanging around on Linkedin, or say hi here.
